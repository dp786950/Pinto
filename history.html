Our Journey Through Time

Early Origins

The Pinto family traces its roots to Dodowa in Accra. Our ancestors established strong values of unity, respect, and family support that continue to guide the family today.

Great Grandparents Era

The great grandparents laid the foundation of the family's traditions and leadership structure, bringing together relatives from different communities.

Growth of the Family

Over the years the Pinto family expanded as children, grandchildren and relatives grew the family tree, strengthening bonds and shared identity.

Modern Generation

Today the Pinto (BiLHaT) family continues to preserve its heritage while embracing modern life, keeping traditions alive through reunions, meetings and shared activities.
